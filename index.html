Research Interests & Projects

Predicting the time to relapse for the GBM patients.

Predicting Tumor Spheroid Status Through the Lens of Radiomics Analysis of Post-Treatment Dynamics.

Exploring radiomics-based approaches to assess and predict tumor spheroid behavior post-treatment.

Inverse analysis of biopsies using mathematical models as a virtual reality.

Utilizing computational models to reconstruct biopsy data and enhance diagnostics.

Modeling the Role of Tumor Cell Cluster Emission in Metastasis Formation.

Investigating tumor cell clusters and their contribution to metastasis formation using mathematical simulations.
